About Ashu AI

This AI-powered chatbot was developed as a hands-on project during my participation in the IBM Edunet Workshop. It integrates IBM Watson Assistant to provide an interactive conversational experience. Built using HTML, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, IBM Cloud and Watson X AI .
The chatbot features a draggable, resizable, and mobile-responsive UI, complete with a visitor counter and animated background for an engaging user experience.
